An industry-first service designed for women travellers.

Vistara is thrilled to present #VistaraWomanFlyer. An industry-first service designed especially for our women travelers. As part of this unique initiative, female passengers can choose their desired seat from among a window or an aisle seat, while female passengers traveling alone will additionally enjoy special baggage and transport assistance on arrival, completely free of cost.
To enjoy this service, passengers can pre-select their preferred seat during booking, after booking (through the ‘manage my booking’ feature) as well as during web check-in. If the passenger was unable to book the seat, we will ensure only a window or an aisle seat is assigned to the passenger at check-in. Book tickets to become a Vistara WomanFlyer today!

We also provide sanitary pads on board our flight to our women travellers on request. These are ISO 9001:2015 certified, bio-degradable and organic sanitary napkins made from plant-based fibres that are free of plastic, toxins and perfume. This initiative makes us the first domestic airline to offer sanitary pads on flights within India.
\ Recognizing the need for this essential amenity, we have introduced this service to assure women travellers of a comfortable flying experience every time they fly Vistara.
Please get in touch with the cabin crew on your Vistara flight should you wish to opt for the service.
